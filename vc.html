VC Investments and Strategic Partnerships

Paranet is actively open to venture capital funding and strategic partnerships that align with our mission to reshape how the web works—making it more private, independent, and user-controlled. We are looking to collaborate with visionary firms, angel investors, and forward-thinking individuals who share our belief in building a para-centralized, algorithm-free digital space.

If you or your organization are interested in investing in Paranet or exploring partnership opportunities, please reach out to us at caspian_gage@proton.me. We’d be excited to discuss how you can be part of the future of the web.

Paranet version one test launch will be conducted on June 5th, 2025. Those who want to have early access of the network must join the waitlist.
